Loba Short film project
"LOBA" is a short film written by Patricia de Andrés and Julia Lucía Vargas. For this project, we have wirtten and script, studied a possible cast and taken narrative and visual references for its preproduction. Moreover, we have developed a filming calendar with different locations and set a production calendar with a crew. After fixing the budget and the funding channels, we are currently searching for a production and distributing company who wishes to participate in the creation of “LOBA”.
