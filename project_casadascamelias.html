Casa das Camelias Brand design
I have always felt a deep devotion to Galician pazos. The stone façades, the warm lanterns at their entrances, the enormous wooden doors, the green gardens with secluded corners to stroll through, the porticos, the interior courtyards with a porous stone fountain, the moss, the history they hold... This project is a love letter to all these places and a creative challenge to learn more about the branding behind the tourism projects of Galicia's pazos and paradores. To this end, I have created a fictional inn called "Casa das Camelias", which I wanted to include some of my favourite features of these places: cosy accommodation, a restaurant serving local produce and its own vineyard. With this idea in mind, I developed a brand book from scratch using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign, in which I proposed a complete brand identity: logo, isotype, colour palette, fonts, and adaptation to its applications in menus, bottle labels, business cards, among others.
