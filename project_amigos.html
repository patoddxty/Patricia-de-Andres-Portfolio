App Amigos del Prado Digital development project
App Amigos Museo del Prado is a mobile application design project that adapts the Amigos del Museo del Prado platform to the mobile environment, with the aim of facilitating access to content and services from the Museum itself. The app is designed for use in the context of a visit, prioritising accessibility, usability and immediacy. The project incorporates features such as access to digital membership cards via QR codes, a calendar of cultural activities, tour guides and course tracking, enhancing the in-person experience and strengthening the bond between the Museum and its community of Friends.
