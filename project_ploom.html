Ploom Motion graphics
Collaboration with Darwin & Verne agency in the development of motion graphics pieces for the Ploom Aura brand, working in coordination with their creative team. My work focused on animating the graphic elements of the advertisement and adapting the pieces to different formats and durations for digital distribution. The animations were created in Adobe After Effects, strictly following the brand's brand book. The project included adapting content to different international markets, adjusting texts, rhythms and screen times, as well as optimising formats according to different sizes and viewing platforms.
