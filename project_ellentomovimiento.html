El Lento Movimiento Album cover illustration
"El Lento Movimiento" is a musical project that reflects on personal growth, nostalgia for childhood, and constant curiosity about what lies ahead. Based on a discourse linked to the passage of time—and taking into account the artist's connection to nuclear physics and cosmic rays—the visual proposal is supported by a retro-inspired spatial aesthetic. The ellipses orbiting around the protagonist represent the cyclical and temporal movement in which he is symbolically trapped. The illustration was developed entirely in Adobe Illustrator, creating the artist's vector silhouette, elliptical compositions and lettering using custom typography, carefully balancing concept, form and visual narrative.
