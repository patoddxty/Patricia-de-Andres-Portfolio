Saturday night fever 3D Enviroment Design
Saturday Night Fever is a 3D virtual space design project inspired by the iconic clubs and discos of the 1970s and 1980s, drawing on the visual imagery of disco funk. The aim of the project is to recreate an immersive and vibrant atmosphere through the use of materials, lighting and spatial composition. All elements of the space were modelled in Autodesk 3ds Max, with customised textures such as marble, velvet, methacrylate and steel. Lighting plays a key role in the project, using neon lights of different colours and intensities to reinforce the nocturnal and dynamic character of the space.
