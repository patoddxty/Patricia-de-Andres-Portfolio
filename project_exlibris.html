Exlibris Illustration
This design began as a personal gift idea. As an artist, I take every opportunity to give someone a design of my own that I believe reflects their personality. I also find personalised illustration to be a wonderful creative challenge, capturing someone's essence in a few simple, aesthetic strokes. In this project, I also wanted to adapt a typeface to personalise it to the exact tone of the message, with a retro style similar to a traditional Galician font. When illustrating, it is important to consider both the format in which the design itself will be encapsulated and the message we want it to convey. An ex-libris is related to an interest in reading, attention to detail and care for our books, which become ‘ours’ beyond ownership, but because of what they have meant to us. This project was developed using Adobe Illustrator, carrying out the vector illustration and layout of a customised typeface.
