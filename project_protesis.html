Prótesis Rock band merchandising design
Logo and brand image design for a personal creative project involving a rock band. For this project, I created illustrations for merchandising with the aim of supporting the band's launch and creating their visual identity. I designed stickers, flyers and the holy grail of every indie band: T-shirts to hand out at their concerts. The composition of the Prótesis isotype (original name: Hip Prosthesis) consists of a group of bones (some of them broken) together with screws, forming a star shape. For the group, the star symbolises the illusion of artistic growth and the bond that unites them, having all met in Madrid (whose flag is made up of seven stars). The project was carried out using Illustrator to create the vector shapes of the stars and bones, and Photoshop to arrange the composition of vectors and labels.
