Arrival Album cover illustration
This proposal visually reinterprets the symbolic universe of Denis Villeneuve's Arrival, focusing on the circular shapes and graphic codes that articulate the film's narrative. The composition seeks to convey mystery, silence, and depth, respecting the minimalist and contemplative atmosphere of the film through a clean and balanced visual language. The illustration was created in Adobe Illustrator, working on the vector shapes and overall composition, and then laid out in Adobe InDesign to adapt it to the album cover format. The project focuses on formal synthesis and careful use of negative space as key elements of the visual proposal.
