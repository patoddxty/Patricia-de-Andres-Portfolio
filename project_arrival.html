Arrival Album cover illustration
Collaboration with Sushita to record and edit audiovisual content for social media during an event held in the gardens of the Palacio de Liria. My work focused on covering the filming of the event, capturing both the atmosphere of the space and the product and the audience's experience. I then edited various pieces in formats adapted to social media, respecting the visual identity and tone of the brand. The result was a series of dynamic and aesthetically pleasing videos, designed to maximise their impact and engagement on digital platforms.
